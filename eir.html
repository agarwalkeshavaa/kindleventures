Kindle Venture’s – EIR Programme

Overview

Pioneering expedition for extraordinary individuals to build together world class start-ups. Kindle EIR programme is excited to invite and assist brilliant technical minds through the cohort. This program is designed for strong technical people with deep insights to thrive and foster in Indian and US Market. It is intended to build & promote world class Tech start-ups from India.

We would like to bring our decades of experience in Investment management, financial services and technology industries in the United States & India put to use for laying the foundation of world class enterprises. EIR cohort is intended to create assimilation of techno geeks as part of the founding teams for various start-ups. Kindle's careful orchestration of talent, hard work and experience will chisel the best technical co-founders and will lay the foundation of impacting enterprises from India.

Our Offerings




Who should apply?

IF YOU HAVE THE FOLLOWING CRITERIAS:
(1)Strong Technical & Coding Background
(2)Can Work Full Time
(3)Enthusiastic To Develop Software products and solutions
(4)Willingness to start an entrepreneurial journey
You might have just landed at the right place

What is not mandatory to apply?

We intend to break the Psychological stigma for brilliant minds which makes it difficult for them to try entrepreneurship journey.
It is not necessary to have it all
Entrepreneurship is a journey of self discovery and development. We plan to teach entrepreneurship to super enthusiastic individuals who has exceptional tech talent and ready to devote themselves for the vision.
