About

We are a technology first investment platform setup with the objective to help propel software product startups in India and the United States. We have three key areas:

Mentoring (Incubator/Accelerator)

We are working very selectively with a few startups with exceptional management teams to help them shape their product and build a better value proposition. Our incubation team consists of former entrepreneurs both in India and United states that have built software products and started, scaled, and sold companies to Fortune 100 technology companies. You are welcome to apply for the mentorship program.

EIR (Entrepreneur-in-Residence)

In our investment experience, we came across phenomenal technology entrepreneurs or single founder technology companies which have a strong product/idea but lack the ability to build a strong team and define the market opportunity. In most cases such companies or entrepreneurs are not able to build scalable organizations or attract external capital. We are trying to fill this gap through our EIR program by combining a technology entrepreneur with a market facing entrepreneur to build a more holistic team and provide our network and relationships to help build an early stage startup. You can apply for our EIR program.

Early Stage (Seed) Funding

We are looking for early stage software technology product startups which have a balanced team, a compelling software technology product, and a large market opportunity with some proven traction. You are welcome to apply for our funding program by filing a short application.

Our Background

Kindle Ventures (an incubator) is a part of Kentrus India Fund, a SEBI CAT-I, AIF. The fund managers have independently spent over 20 years in a combination of investment management, financial services and technology industries in the United States and India. The fund managers have managed an earlier fund of ~$100M delivering over 20% gross annualized IRR and a proprietary technology investment record of 3-4x MOIC. The fund management team personnel have been a part of established private equity funds, management team members of companies with strategic $1B+ exits, and startup entrepreneurs in the Silicon Valley. The fund is also advised and guided by a strong set of advisors and directors that have served as board members in Fortune 100 Indian companies, led some of the largest institutions as managing directors and chairmen and have strong expertise in alternative investments.
