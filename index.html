About

We are a technology first investment platform setup to help propel software startups in India and the United States. We have three key areas of operations:

Mentoring

We are working very selectively with a few startups with exceptional management teams to help them shape their product and build a better value proposition. You are welcome to apply for the mentorship program.

Early Stage Funding

We are looking for early stage technology startups which have a balanced team, a compelling product, and a phenomenal market potential. You are welcome to apply for our funding program by filing a short application.

EIR (Entrepreneur-in-Residence)

In our investment experience, we come across phenomenal technology entrepreneurs or single founder technology companies which have a strong product but capability to build a strong product but lack the ability to build a strong team and define the market opportunity. In most cases such companies or entrepreneurs do not get funding. We are trying to fill this gap through our EIR program by combining a technology entrepreneur with a market facing entrepreneur to build a more holistic team and provide our network and relationships to help build an early stage startup apply for the Program.

.

Fund Background

Kindle Ventures is a part of Kentrus India Fund, a SEBI CAT-I, AIF. The fund managers have independently spent over 20 years in a combination of investment management, financial services and technology industries in the United States and India. The fund managers have managed an earlier fund of ~$100M delivering over 20% gross annualized IRR and a proprietary technology investment record of 3-4x MOIC. The fund management team personnel have been a part of established private equity funds, management team members of companies with strategic $1B+ exits, and startup entrepreneurs in the Silicon Valley. The fund is also advised and guided by a strong set of advisors and directors that have served as board members in Fortune 100 Indian companies, led some of the largest institutions as managing directors and chairmen and have strong expertise in alternative investments.

The core strategy of the fund is to focus on technology startups with exceptional teams and compelling use cases in areas of expertise and domain knowledge of the core team. Our key areas of team expertise include financial services, agriculture, and machine learning/ artificial intelligence.

We fundamentally believe that indigenous startups (MSME’s) in India with the right mentorship and capital can become large multinational companies. MSME’s will be at the forefront of steadily disrupting all industries such as banking, healthcare, education etc. Our goal is to invest in such disruptions through a combination of equity and convertible structures in ventures where we can bring strategic insight, strong governance, relationships for scale.
