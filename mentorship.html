Kindle Venture’s – Mentorship Programme

Overview

Kindle mentorship programme is excited to assist brilliant minds through its mentorship cohort. This program is uniquely designed for Indian Startups with deep insights to Indian and US Market. The Program is intended to build sustainable ecosystem to promote and create evolution of world class Tech Startups from India.
|We would like to bring our decades of experience in Investment management, financial services and technology industries in the United States & India put to use for laying the foundation of world class enterprises. Cohort is intended to create assimilation for startups in the segments wherein the kindle team has advances as top-tier. Agritech | Fintech | AI/ML | Social Impact - These tech segments are going to shape our future and thus requires focused attention, as against nurturing a conventional tech startup.

Why Mentorship is required?


When you start your entrepreneurship journey there is too much to do in too less a time. During these initial days Founders do everything by themselves and often loose the track between IMPORTANT and URGENT task. We at Kindle handhold your entrepreneurial journey and makes you part of our high energy team who helps you in laying the foundation for a great enterprise. We not only advise but walk, run, fall and succeed together.

Who fits best for our mentorship Programme?

Early stage Founder who is spending sleepless nights in building a world class product.
If these are some of the challenges that is bothering you every day for instance – Unable to identify the right cofounder !!
Still struggling to get the PMF (product market fit) for your product !!
You have a product and few customers but you don’t know how to scale !!
You don’t know how to raise funds !!
You are still clueless about forming a Board of Director and Board of Advisors who can spearhead your organization !!
You might have just landed at the right place.

What we do ?

We are not a StartUp school. We are here to do and make things happen along with passionate founders. We provide customize one on one support to each founder and chalk out a path to a successful enterprise. We will be deeply involved in the operation of the business and will help the founder to take the moonshot. And when you are ready we will be there to Invest in you.
